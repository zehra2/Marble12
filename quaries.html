QUARRIES AND OPERATIONS

Quarry means an area where an excavation or excavation system is carried out with the aim of sorting quarry minerals (either in their natural form or in a state of dissolution). “Marble Arts" quarries are enforced with advanced technology equipment.

Look at our Quarries

Marble Arts main supply sources are its own quarries and preferential supply agreements with different quarries enable us to manage large-scale projects through the guaranteed supply of materials. The company has its own marble and granite deposits located in Nothern Areas and Balochistan province of Pakistan, where the best quarry of White Marble is located in the region of Ziarat; holds the finest white texture marble known as White Ziarat.
Marble Arts is offering a broad range of unique and exclusive natural stone facilitating the ideal choice of material.
